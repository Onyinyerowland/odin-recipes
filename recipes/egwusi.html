Egwusi Soup Egwusi Soup

Description

This soup combines uziza Leaves, pumpkin leaves, Melon seeds also known as egwusi, meat and seafood. Egwusi Soup is a traditional dish eaten in every tribes in Nigeria

You can use garri, pounded yam, fufu, wheat flour or semovita to enjoy this delicacy

Ingredients

  • Dried Fish
  • Cow Meat
  • 2 tablespoons Crayfish
  • 10 to 15cl Palm-oil
  • Maggi cubes
  • Onions
  • Uziza leaves
  • pumpkin leaves
  • Stock fish
  • melon seeds or egwusi
  • Kanda
  • Goat Meat
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Habanero peppers

Steps in preparing Egusi Soup

  1. Grind or pound the melon seeds or eguwsi
  2. Grind or pound pepper and crayfish and cut the onions into tiny pieces
  3. Slice the uziza and pumpkin leaves into tny pieces
  4. Boil the beaf, goat meat,kanda and stock fish with the diced onions, stock cubes or maggi and salt in very little quantity of water for 5mins
  5. Add the dried fish and cook for 1 min
  6. Add palm-oil, crayfish and pepper to the stock
  7. Once it starts boiling, add the blended egwusi and cook for 20 mins
  8. Add seasoning and salt to taste and cook for additional 5 mins
  9. Egwusi soup is ready
  10. Serve with any swallow of choice
