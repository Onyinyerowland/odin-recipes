Afang Soup Recepies Afang Soup

Description

This soup combines okazi Leaves, water leaves, meat and seafood. Specifically, Afang soup is a traditional dish of the Efik and Ibibio tribes in Nigeria

You can use garri, pounded yam, fufu, wheat flour or semovita to enjoy this delicacy

Ingredients

  • Dried Fish
  • Cow Meat
  • 2 tablespoons Crayfish
  • 20 to 30cl Palm-oil
  • Maggi cubes
  • Onions
  • 250g Water-leaf
  • 400g Afang-leaf
  • Stock fish
  • Periwinkle
  • Kanda
  • Goat Meat
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Habanero peppers

Steps in preparing Afang Soup

  1. Wash, drain and slice the water leaves into tiny pieces.
  2. Grind or pound the sliced afang leaves
  3. Grind or pound pepper and crayfish and cut the onions into tiny pieces
  4. Boil the beaf, goat meat,kanda and stock fish with the diced onions, stock cubes or maggi and salt in very little quantity of water for 5mins
  5. Add the dried fish and cook for 1 min
  6. Add palm-oil, crayfish and pepper to the stock
  7. Once it starts boiling, add the afang leave, waterleaf and periwinkle
  8. Cook for 5 to 10 mins and add seasoning and salt to taste and cook for additional 5 mins
  9. Afang soup is ready
  10. Serve with any swallow of choice
