Ogbono Soup Ogbono Soup

Description

This soup combines uziza Leaves, pumpkin leaves, meat, ogbono and seafood. Ogbono soup is a traditional dish of several tribes in Nigeria

You can use garri, pounded yam, fufu, wheat flour or semovita to enjoy this delicacy

Ingredients

  • Dried Fish
  • Cow Meat
  • 2 tablespoons Crayfish
  • 5 to 10cl Palm-oil
  • Maggi cubes
  • Onions
  • Pumpkin-leaf
  • Uziza-leaf
  • Stock fish
  • Ogbono
  • Kanda
  • Goat Meat
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Habanero peppers

Steps in preparing Ogbono Soup

  1. Wash, drain and slice the uziza and pumpkin leaves into tiny pieces.
  2. Grind or pound pepper and crayfish and cut the onions into tiny pieces
  3. Boil the beaf, goat meat,kanda and stock fish with the diced onions, stock cubes or maggi and salt in very little quantity of water for 5mins
  4. Add the dried fish and cook for 1 min
  5. Add palm-oil, crayfish and pepper to the stock
  6. Once it starts boiling, add ogbono allow to cook for 10 -15 mins, add uziza leaves and pumpkin leaves
  7. Cook for 5 to 10 mins and add seasoning and salt to taste and cook for additional 5 mins
  8. Ogbono soup is ready
  9. Serve with any swallow of choice
